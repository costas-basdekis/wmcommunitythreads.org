William Morris Community Threads (WMCT) is successor organisation to William Morris Big Local (WMBL)

We are William Morris Community Threads, a new community organisation in Walthamstow. We bring people together and work alongside you to weave stronger connections in our community.

Inspired by the community achievements of William Morris Big Local, which comes to an end in 2025 after 12 years, we will continue its legacy of positive change.

Sign up for our mailing list to stay in touch or get in touch through our contact form to find out more.
