William Morris Community Threads (WMCT) is the successor organisation to William Morris Big Local (WMBL)

Inspired by the community achievements of William Morris Big Local over the last 12 years, as that programme finishes in 2025, we will be here to continue its legacy of positive change.

We are William Morris Community Threads, a new community organisation based in the William Morris area of Walthamstow. We bring people together and work alongside you to weave stronger connections in our community.

Sign up for our mailing list to keep in touch or complete our contact form to find out more and get involved.
